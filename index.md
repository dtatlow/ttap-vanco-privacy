# Privacy Policy – PlasmaBind – Vancomycin PK Simulator

Last updated: February 2026

PlasmaBind – Vancomycin PK Simulator is an educational pharmacokinetic (PK) simulation application developed by PlasmaBind Inc., a Canadian corporation.

This application is intended for research, education, and visualization purposes only. The application does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or clinical decision support.

This Privacy Policy applies to the iOS and Android versions of PlasmaBind – Vancomycin PK Simulator.

---

## Data Collection

PlasmaBind – Vancomycin PK Simulator does **NOT** collect, store, transmit, or share:

- Personal health information (PHI)
- Patient identifiers
- User personal data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics data

All calculations and simulations are performed locally and offline on the user’s device.

No information entered into the application is transmitted to external servers or third-party services.

---

## Data Storage

Information entered into the application is stored temporarily in device memory only during active use of the application.

- No data is saved to cloud services  
- No user accounts are created  
- No persistent databases are used  

All entered data is cleared when the application is closed, restarted, or refreshed.

---

## Analytics and Tracking

PlasmaBind – Vancomycin PK Simulator does **NOT** use:

- Analytics services  
- Tracking technologies  
- Advertising networks  
- Third-party SDKs  
- External data collection tools  

---

## Permissions

PlasmaBind – Vancomycin PK Simulator does **NOT** request access to:

- Location services  
- Contacts  
- Camera  
- Microphone  
- Background tracking services  

---

## Clipboard Usage

The application may allow users to copy simulation summaries to the device clipboard.

Users are responsible for ensuring that copied content does not contain patient identifiers or sensitive information.

---

## Children’s Privacy

PlasmaBind – Vancomycin PK Simulator is intended for adult healthcare learners and professionals.

The application is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age and does not knowingly collect data from minors.

---

## Regulatory and Clinical Use Disclaimer

PlasmaBind – Vancomycin PK Simulator is not a regulated medical device.

The application is not intended to replace:

- Clinical judgment  
- Therapeutic drug monitoring  
- Institutional protocols  
- Professional medical advice  

Users must independently validate simulation outputs against measured laboratory results and local clinical guidelines prior to any clinical use.
